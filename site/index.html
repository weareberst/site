Fee-for-service

You invest your capital in return for our expertise.

The traditional agency model, fee-for-service is a good match for established organisations looking to explore new markets and incubate new ideas. Or, investors who recognise the need for external design expertise to unlock the potential of a venture.

Equity-for-service

We invest our expertise in exchange for equity in your venture.

​​Created for early-stage businesses hoping to get investment-ready or take the next big leap forward when capital is not yet abundant. Comes in various flavours (equity, profit or royalty share) and is often combined with fee-for-service.

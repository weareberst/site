We’re committed to supporting innovation from all backgrounds and operate a flexible approach to funding. Alongside traditional fee-for-service projects, we welcome equity-exchange and revenue-share terms.

Fee-for-service

You invest your capital in return for our expertise

The traditional agency model, fee-for-service is a good match for established organisations looking to explore new markets and incubate new ideas. Or, investors who recognise the need for external design expertise to unlock the potential in their portfolio.

Equity-for-service

We invest our expertise in exchange for equity.

​​Created for early-stage businesses looking to become investment-ready or take the next leap forward, even though capital is not yet abundant. Comes in various flavours (equity, profit/royalty share) and can also be combined with fee-for-service

Combining the spirit of a startup with the creativity of a design studio, we like to get down in the trenches, work alongside our partners and turn fledgling ideas into pioneering digital businesses.

Founders James Bates and James Box have worked in the design industry for two decades, helping organisations deliver and grow high-profile, breakthrough digital products. Their sweet spot has always been rapid innovation and in 2018 they narrowed their focus and created Berst.

Born as a venture design studio, we are at our best partnering with individuals and teams with the courage and determination to think differently. Those prepared to go beyond the familiar and take a brave leap forward.
